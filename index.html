Water Resistance

The iPhone 13 Pro and Pro Max feature an IP68 water resistant rating. The smartphones are able to withstand a depth of up to six meters (19.7 feet) for up to 30 minutes, identical to the iPhone 12 Pro models.In the IP68 number, the 6 refers to dust resistance ,With the IP68 water resistance rating, the iPhone 13 Pro can hold up to splashes, rain, and accidental water exposure, but intentional water exposure should be avoided if possible.
